It's safe to say most information was lost during this process, however interestingly there are still elements present that hint at the original piece. For example you can pick up on what chord/key the piece could be from the certain notes played at specific points, but overall I feel this is only obvious after listening to the original, had someone listened to this exported MIDI file first they'd probably not be able to take much away from it. In regards to the sheet music transcription, it's not gotten anything correct, and seems to think the piece is in 3/4. I wouldn't say this is a good place to start for someone needing help to transcribe a piece, starting from scratch would be more beneficial in my opinion.
