MuseScore OMR Engine Transcription

You can download the corrected MuseScore file here.


The first thing I noticed was it couldn't transcribe the drum notation. Drums - specifically kicks and snares - are of course "tuned" instruments, however I'm assuming the algorithms that the OMR engine uses to transcribe are perhaps geared towards melodic instruments. If that's the case then I can understand why this didn't transcribe well.


Some other changes and issues encountered were:


  • Incorrect time signatures, none of the 7/4 bars were present and everything was presented as 4/4
  • Some bars were just completely empty, filled with only a rest
  • Incorrect instrumentation, all instruments were replaced with a default "voice" part
  • No articulations were recognised, such as trills and vibrato
  • No title/artist information recognised by the OMR engine
