Finally, with respect to key signatures, MusicXML expresses them through the 'key' element with its sub-elements 'fifths' and 'mode', but MEI uses the 'keySig' element to express the same. This difference results in MusicXML being better suited for reading, while MEI is better suited for quick parsing.
